Help & Guide

Round Setup

Set up your round here before entering scores. Add course info, choose round options, and enter player names. The scorecard updates right away as you make changes.

Course Name

Enter your course name. It is saved with the round so you can find it quickly in Saved Rounds.

Date

Choose the round date. The date is saved and shown with the round in Saved Rounds.

Holes to Play

Choose 9 or 18 holes. If you choose 9 holes, only that side is used for scoring and totals.

Starting Hole

Pick Front 9 (Hole 1) or Back 9 (Hole 10). The scorecard order and totals follow the side you start on.

Number of Players

Choose how many players are in the round. Only that many player name fields are shown.

Player Names

Enter each player name. A player appears on the scorecard once a real name is entered.

Customize Pars & Prevent Accidental Edits

Use "Customize Pars (Optional)" only if your course has different pars. Turn on "Prevent accidental edits" after entering scores to lock inputs and avoid accidental changes.

Save and Reset

Tap "Save Round" to store the round on your phone and download an updated spreadsheet (.csv). The file includes hole-by-hole values plus Front 9, Back 9, and Total for both Strokes and Putts. Tap "Reset Current Round" to clear setup and scores and start over.

Saved Rounds and Spreadsheet Files

Saved Rounds shows your previously saved rounds. Every time you tap "Save Round", an updated spreadsheet file is downloaded to your device. Keep those files if you want an extra backup, because browser storage can be lost during cleanup, app reinstalls, or device changes.
